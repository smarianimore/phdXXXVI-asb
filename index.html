About the content

    The course is a general introduction to the two mentioned emergent IT topics

    Technical content limited to practical examples

    Goal: make you aware of what these technologies are, and how they can help design modern software systems

Part I

  • Autonomous systems: an overview
    1. On the notion of autonomy
    2. Agents
    3. Multiagent systems
    4. Game theory: individual stance
    5. Game theory: collective stance
    6. Multiagent interactions
    7. Conclusion

Part II

  • Blockchain: an overview
    1. Why
    2. What
    3. How
    4. Deep dive: Ethereum
    5. Deep dive: smart contracts
    6. Conclusion

Final examination

    You choose:

    • fill a quiz (multiple choice + open question)
    • compile a brief essay on any topic you enjoyed
      • may it help your research activity? How?
      • support your claims with references to scientific literature
      • 2-3 pages, two-columns format
    • play with any technology mentioned, e.g.:
      • program a JADE/Jason agent, or MAS
      • simulate some emergent phenomena with NetLogo
      • install & run any blockchain
      • write and deploy a Ethereum smart contract
      • propose your own "project"
